    • Influenza Vaccine

    Recommended annually for all adults, especially those with underlying health conditions.

    Td Vaccine (Tetanus, Diphtheria)

    Booster doses recommended every 10 years.

    Tdap Vaccine (Tetanus, Diphtheria, Pertussis)

    Recommended during each pregnancy and for adults who have not previously received it.

    HPV Vaccine

    Catch-up vaccination recommended for females aged 27-45 years who have not been previously vaccinated.

    MMR Vaccine

    Catch-up vaccination recommended for women born after 1957 who have not received two doses.

    Varicella Vaccine

    Catch-up vaccination recommended for adults who have not had chickenpox or received the vaccine.
