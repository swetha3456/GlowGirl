      Health Check Up

    • Regular Physical Examinations

    Annual check-ups with a healthcare provider are crucial for monitoring overall health.

    Blood Pressure Screening

    Regular blood pressure checks are important to monitor cardiovascular health.

    Cervical Cancer Screening

    Starting at age 21, women should undergo regular cervical cancer screenings with Pap smears.

    Breast Self-Examinations

    Regular breast self-examinations are valuable for detecting any changes or abnormalities in breast tissue.

    Sexually Transmitted Infections (STIs) Screening

    Regular STI screenings are essential for detecting infections such as chlamydia, gonorrhea, and HIV.
