    • Hepatitis B Vaccine

    Usually given at birth, followed by additional doses during infancy.

    DTaP Vaccine (Diphtheria, Tetanus, Pertussis)

    Given in a series of doses during infancy, childhood, and adolescence.

    Polio Vaccine

    Typically administered in multiple doses during infancy and early childhood.

    MMR Vaccine (Measles, Mumps, Rubella)

    Given in two doses, usually around 12-15 months and 4-6 years of age.

    Varicella (Chickenpox) Vaccine

    Administered in two doses, usually around 12-15 months and 4-6 years of age.

    HPV Vaccine (Human Papillomavirus)

    Recommended for females aged 9-26 years, given in a series of doses.
