    High Knees

    High knees is a cardiovascular exercise where you rapidly lift your knees towards your chest alternately, promoting agility and engaging multiple muscle groups including the core and hip flexors.

    Toe Touches

    Toe touches are a bodyweight exercise that strengthens your core and improves flexibility by bending at the hips and reaching towards your toes.
